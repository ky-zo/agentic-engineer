# AI Shipping Offer Strategy

Date: 2026-03-24

## Core Thesis

Do not lead with "premium AI consulting for companies."

Lead with a narrower, more credible promise:

**Help software companies become AI-native shipping teams.**

This is closer to the strongest proof point from HeyReach and easier to explain, price, and sell.

## Best Initial Market

Primary buyer:
- Founder / CEO / CTO of a software company with an existing product team

Primary situation:
- They know AI matters
- Their product, engineering, and QA teams still ship the old way
- People are experimenting with tools like ChatGPT, but there is no unified operating model
- The company is slower than it should be because AI adoption is fragmented or stalled

Secondary market for later:
- Early-stage founders who want to build with AI from zero to one

Recommendation:
- Start with existing software companies first
- Treat zero-to-one founder help as phase two, not the main launch message

## Sharp Problem Statement

Companies do not need more AI demos or a strategy PDF.

They need someone who can step into the team, show what is actually possible, train the team on concrete workflows, and get AI adopted in a way that changes shipping speed.

## Positioning Statement

For founder-led software companies whose teams know AI matters but still ship too slowly, this offer is an AI shipping sprint that wires AI into product, engineering, and QA workflows.

Unlike generic AI consultants or strategy decks, the work happens inside the team's real stack and real workflows, with concrete adoption and implementation support.

## Offer Structure

### 1. AI Shipping Sprint

- Length: 2 weeks
- Format: 4 workshops
- Price: $10,000

Target outcome:
- Team setup and alignment
- Repeatable AI workflows for engineering and QA
- Tooling rollout for daily use
- One or two real automations or implementation wins
- Clear next-step roadmap

This is the cleanest first offer because it matches the HeyReach case and is already validated.

### 2. Fractional AI Shipping Lead

- Price: $10,000 to $15,000 per month
- Structure: limited seats

Target outcome:
- Ongoing implementation support
- Removal of adoption bottlenecks
- Workflow optimization across product, engineering, QA, and operations
- Higher-leverage use of AI across the company over time

This is the upsell after the sprint.

## Role Framing

Best role framing:

**Fractional AI Shipping Lead**

This is stronger than "consultant" because it suggests embedded execution, not advice-only work.
